How to use the site

  1. First, Login or create an account on Caffine Tracker.
  2. At the start page, you will see a list of caffiene products you have consumed
  3. To add to the list, Add the name of the drink, the size, amount of caffeine(in mg), and the date
  4. When you are finished, click submit.
  5. Your Entry is then added to the your databse, where you can track it and how much caffeine you ingest thruout the year.
